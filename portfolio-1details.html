Project detail

In the digital age, educational institutions need robust systems to manage their operations, especially when it comes to handling fees and financial transactions. SchoolPayment is a digital platform that streamlines the process of school fee management by connecting administrators, students, and parents in an integrated environment.

Technology Used


• Front-End Technologies:

        ReactJS, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript


• Back-End Technologies:

        Django Rest Framework, Json Web Token(JWT)


• Database:

        MicroSoft SQL (MSSQL)


• Payment Gateway Integration:

        Paystack


Usage:


1. Administrative Setup:

Registration*: Before utilizing the platform, school administrators are required to register. This ensures that only authorized personnel have access to sensitive financial data.

Confirmation Email*: Upon successful registration, administrators receive a confirmation email. This email provides them with login details, ensuring a second layer of security.

Initial Login and Password Change*: Security is a top priority for SchoolPayment. When administrators log in for the first time using details from the confirmation email, they are prompted to change their password. This feature prevents misuse in case the confirmation email is accessed by unauthorized individuals.


2. Student and Parent Integration:

Registration*: Similar to administrators, students and parents need to register to gain access to the platform. This process ensures each user is uniquely identifiable, providing a clear path of communication and accountability.

Confirmation and Password Change*: To mirror the security measures in place for administrators, students and parents also receive confirmation emails post-registration. They are similarly prompted to change their password upon their first login.


3. Fee Creation and Management:

Fee Setup by Admin*: SchoolPayment allows administrators to create and manage various fee categories. This flexibility ensures that the platform can handle different types of fees, be it tuition, extracurricular activities, or lab fees.

Dashboard Integration*: Once a fee is set up by the administrator, it is automatically reflected in the respective dashboards of students and parents. For instance, if a student is part of a particular extracurricular activity, the associated fee will show up in their dashboard as an "outstanding fee." Parents, having a birdâ€™s eye view, will see the cumulative fees for all their enrolled children.


4. Payments:

Initiating Payments*: Both students and parents can initiate payments directly from their dashboards. This feature eliminates the need for manual fee payment, making the process smoother and more efficient.

Payment Status*: Once a payment is made, it doesn't immediately get confirmed. Instead, it gets marked as "awaiting approval." This stage acts as a verification step, ensuring that the transaction was legitimate and successful.


5. Final Approval by Admin*:

Reviewing Payments*: Payments made by students or parents come to the administrators for a final review. This manual oversight is a crucial step to avoid potential financial discrepancies.

Approval and Confirmation*: If the payment is deemed legitimate, administrators can approve it. Once approved, the payment status changes from "awaiting approval" to "confirmed." This system ensures transparency and accountability in the fee payment process.


*Conclusion*:

SchoolPayment is not just a digital platform; it's a comprehensive solution to the intricate problem of school fee management. By integrating administrators, students, and parents into a single platform, it ensures smooth transactions, transparent communication, and efficient fee management. As educational institutions progress towards a more digital future, SchoolPayment stands as a beacon, showing the way forward.
